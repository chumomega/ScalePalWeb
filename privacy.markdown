# Privacy Policy

At ScalePal, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to keep your data local and private.

**Data Storage:** All your weight tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal data. It is not transmitted to any server.

**Apple Health Integration:** If you choose to connect ScalePal to Apple Health, data is synced directly between the app and the HealthKit store on your device. This data is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

**Analytics:** We do not collect any personal analytics.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
